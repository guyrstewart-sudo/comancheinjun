# Cőmånčhé INjüń — asheville after midnight

The website for **Cőmånčhé INjüń** — night photographer, two wheels, no permission. Asheville after midnight, lit one flash at a time.

**Live site:** https://guyrstewart-sudo.github.io/comancheinjun/

## What it is

A single-page "bar-shaped website" for people out past midnight on purpose. Five rooms:

- **THE ROUTE** — the bike, roaming the night on a loop.
- **THE BUNKER** — the stream when the bike's out, the ghost tape when it isn't. Sign the tab with a name and hang out.
- **THE TAKE** — the prints. Real photos, newest first, blotter attached.
- **THE WALL** — the congregation, ranked by wattage.
- **THE RULES** — three rules and a bicycle.

## How it's built

No build step, no dependencies, no tracking. Hand-written HTML, CSS, and vanilla ES modules — every file loads straight from the browser. Photos come from [@comancheinjun](https://www.instagram.com/comancheinjun/) on Instagram.

Points are jokes, not currency. No purchases, no scarcity, no tricks — just watts and warmth. Get home safe.
